Welcome to our Health and Wellness Hub!

1. Healthy Eating

What kinds of foods and drinks do I need to consume as I age?

  • Consume more nutrient-dense foods and beverages.
  • Consume a well-balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and low-fat dairy.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking an adequate amount of water throughout the day.

2. Mental Simulation

Nurturing Your Mind:

  • Mindfulness and Relaxation Techniques
  • Brain Games
  • Counseling and Support
  • Reading keeps the mind engaged
  • Listening to audiobooks
  • Indulging in hobbies and arts
  • Volunteering

3. Regular Exercise

Nurturing Your Body:

  • Engage in regular physical activity to maintain strength, flexibility, and balance.
  • Participate in Daily Movement Challenges.
  • Practice Chair Yoga for mobility,balance
  • Regular and gentle stretching

4. Regular Health Check-ups

Nurturing Your Body:

  • Schedule regular check-ups with healthcare professionals to monitor and manage any existing health conditions.
  • Stay up-to-date with vaccinations and screenings for conditions like cancer, diabetes, and osteoporosis.
